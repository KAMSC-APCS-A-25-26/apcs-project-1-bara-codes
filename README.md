<span style="font-size: 4em;">THE GREAT PYRAMID AND THE LOST EXPLORER

</span>
<span style="font-size: 2em;">This project is a text-based adventure game written in Java, where the player is a lost explorer that tries to navigate through the Pyramid of Giza. The player has to make important decisions that will affect their chances of survival. The gameplay includes branching choices, an inventory, and random chance. There are multiple endings, all determined by the player's decisions and actions in the adventure. Be careful of what you choose to do and try your best to survive! </span>
